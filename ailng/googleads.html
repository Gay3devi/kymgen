What Are Google Ads?

Google Ads is an online advertising platform that places your business at the top of Google search results and across millions of partner websites. It allows you to attract potential customers instantly by showing relevant ads based on their search behavior, location, interests, and online activity. With Google Ads, you only pay when someone clicks or interacts with your ad, making it one of the most cost-effective marketing tools.
